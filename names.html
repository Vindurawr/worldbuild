Names in Féarruibhren

Names in Féarruibhren are divided in 3 categories. Profession, Given name, and current regent dynasty name.

For example, a Shipbuilder living in Féardan, whose current ruling dynasty is Fílimféar, may have the name of Eínarþumnar Áilinn Fílimféar

(Profession Given name Ruling Dynasty
