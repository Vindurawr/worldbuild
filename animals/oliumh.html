Oliumh

Máirleagh Éirligh

Characteristics

The Oliumh is a very shy bird, mostly active during the sunrise. It sleeps between 5-11 þormn (hour) per Díagh (day) and will burrow the dirt with it's long beak to find any sort of small insect for food. Their mating Mínagh (season) is Mínþoragh, and will make 3-7 eggs per season.

They are in general very bad flyers, their wings are most often too small to lift their bodies for an extended period of time. Only some smaller sub-species have the potential to fly further. Because of this they have developed an acute sense of burrowing.

Environment

Nesting This bird lives in holes underneath trees, burrowing between the roots and the dirt.

Range They live on Féarruibhren with some subspecies reaching further south to the general continent.
